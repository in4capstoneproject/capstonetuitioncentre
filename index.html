Courses We Offer

We specialise in the core curriculum subjects. A secure understanding of these subjects is essential to understanding all other subjects.

Maths

Maths will allow you to develop better problem-solving skills. It can give you the ability to reason and think analytically through a problem.

English

English Language and Literature encourages us to use our imagination and sparks creativity, teaches us essential social and language skills, reading and writing and how to communicate effectively.

Science

Science helps our understanding of the world around us. Everything we know about the universe, from how trees reproduce to what an atom is made up of, is the result of scientific research and experiment. Human progress throughout history has largely been as a result of advances in science.
